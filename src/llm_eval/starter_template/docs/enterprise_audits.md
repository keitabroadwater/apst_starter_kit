# Enterprise APST Audits

The starter kit is designed for a local conference demo. A full APST audit is a deeper
engagement for teams that need evidence about model safety and reliability under repeated
inference.

## What A Full Audit Adds

- Expanded prompt suites mapped to your product, policy, risk register, and user journeys.
- Provider-specific controls for temperature, sampling depth, rate limits, retries, and logging.
- Calibrated judge modes, including rule checks, LLM-as-judge review, and human spot checks.
- Failure-mode analysis by model, prompt family, temperature, product surface, and severity.
- Reproducible artifacts: configs, result tables, executive summary, and technical appendix.
- Recommendations for mitigations, regression tests, and release gates.

## Typical Inputs

- The model or model endpoint you want to test.
- The application context and safety requirements.
- Prompt categories of concern.
- Existing evaluations, policies, or incident examples.
- Constraints on data handling, privacy, deployment region, and report distribution.
